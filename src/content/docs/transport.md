## Orbit Modular System

*One SmartHub — same mount in the car base docks onto the stroller frame. No adapters.*

**Why Orbit:** 360° rotating base (no leaning into the car), deepest EPP foam side impact protection we found, JPMA certified, 60-day free returns direct from orbitbaby.com.

**Why not the bundle:** The bassinet is not certified for overnight sleep. If you don't need it, the bundle price doesn't add up.

**Runner-up:** Nuna Pipa Urbn — lighter (~7 vs ~14 lbs), baseless install, but shorter height range (29" vs 32"), no modular system.
