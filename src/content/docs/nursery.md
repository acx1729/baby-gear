### Owlet Dream Sock

**Tracks baby's pulse rate and oxygen while they sleep.** The peace of mind that lets you actually close your eyes. 1–18 months (6–30 lbs). HSA/FSA eligible.

**Why we picked it:**
- FDA-cleared — first and only baby monitor with that clearance. Same pulse oximetry tech hospitals use.
- Base station glows green when readings are normal. Flashes red and alarms if something leaves preset zones.
- Very accurate in our experience. Works fine alongside the Cradlewise bouncing.

**Practical notes:**
- **Placement matters.** Follow the guide — wrong position = false readings.
- **Range is limited.** Walk too far from the base station and the alarm goes off.
- **Wi-Fi setup:** Only connects on 2.4 GHz. If you have Eero or similar mesh, temporarily disable 5 GHz to pair.

**Cost:** ~$354 (sock + tax + protection plan)

[Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Owlet-Dream-Sock-Indicators-Movement/dp/B09NCGQSY9?th=1) · [Owlet](https://owletcare.com/products/owlet-dream-sock)
