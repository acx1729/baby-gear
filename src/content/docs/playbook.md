## Things We'd Do Differently

### Don't buy a dedicated changing table.

People told us to. We didn't, and we're glad. Most are just cabinet furniture — some are less safe than a sturdy desk. We repurposed a height-adjustable desk and placed the Keekaroo Peanut on top. The height adjusts to whoever is doing the change, it's sturdier than a purpose-built changing table, and when diaper duty is over you still have a desk.

### Baby Brezza Bottle Washer Pro ($260) — skip it.

We bought it. The cleaning is good. But it only fits 4 bottles at a time and a full cycle takes a while. If you're running it 3–4 times a day, you're constantly loading and unloading. A regular dishwasher with a bottle basket works just as well.

### Spectra Synergy Gold Portable — don't buy it.

It lacks control over suction strength — you get preset levels instead of fine-tuning. The non-portable Synergy Gold lets you adjust each side independently. Get the standard Synergy Gold and the eufy wearable for when you need to move around.

### Milk warmers — avoid.

They heat the water around the bottle, not the milk itself. The output temperature varies every time depending on milk volume, starting temp, and bottle material. Glass holds heat longer and overshoots. Thin plastic heats fast but unevenly. There is no setting you can trust to be consistent. Use the Baby Brezza Formula Pro — it dispenses at a set temperature every time. For breast milk, use a food thermometer.
