## Things to Skip

| Item | Price | Why |
|------|-------|-----|
| Dedicated Changing Table | $150–$400 | Less safe than a sturdy desk. Height-adjustable desk is better. |
| Baby Brezza Bottle Washer Pro | $260 | Only fits 4 bottles. Dishwasher + bottle basket works. |
| Spectra Synergy Gold Portable | ~$200 | No fine suction control. Get the standard + eufy wearable. |
| Milk Warmers (any brand) | $30–$60 | Timing-based, not temperature-based. Burn risk. Use Baby Brezza or thermometer. |

### After Birth

| When | Task | Assigned | Hard Deadline? | Notes |
|------|------|----------|----------------|-------|
| N+1 | Ask nurses to demo diaper changing | Both | No | Before discharge. |
